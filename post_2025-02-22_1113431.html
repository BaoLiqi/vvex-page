Refuting "Artificial Intelligence is Breeding a Generation of Illiterate Programmers"

驳《人工智能正在催生一代文盲程序员》

[Refuting "Artificial Intelligence is Breeding a Generation of Illiterate Programmers"]( https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/-m-ITkVRu2ShxkxELIBDKA) ## What is an illiterate programmer? Companies focus on efficiency, only look at the results, and don't care whether your code is hand-coded or AI-generated. Many companies are now laying off a large number of employees, requiring employees to use AI, and those who cannot use AI will be optimized. As programmers in the information industry, they should not regard "hand-coding" as advanced and AI as low-level. If you can't even use basic tools, what are you talking about advanced? So, **what is an illiterate programmer? It is precisely a programmer who cannot use AI.** Finally, I want to say that AI will not only not create illiterate programmers, but will instead raise the overall level of programmers.

[驳《人工智能正在催生一代文盲程序员》]( https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/-m-ITkVRu2ShxkxELIBDKA) ## 什么是文盲程序员？ 企业注重的是效率，只看结果，而不在乎你的代码是手搓的还是 AI 生成的。现在很多企业大量裁员，要求员工必须使用 AI ，不会用 AI 的就会被优化。 而作为信息行业的程序员，更不应该视“手搓”为高级，AI 为低级。你连基础的工具都不会用，还谈什么高级？ 所以，**什么是文盲程序员？恰恰是不会用 AI 的程序员。** 最后我想说，AI 不仅不会造就文盲程序员，反而会整体拉高程序员的水平。

Moreover, my assertion is that even if AI has developed to the point of having such capabilities, it will still require human assistance and guarantees to complete the coding work before human mathematical capabilities can fully understand this chaotic system.   • Looking back on the history of the human industrial revolution, did each technological innovation eliminate the previous technical workers? Then no, technological innovation has brought various technical tools, which has greatly improved people's work efficiency, and the development of productivity has promoted changes in production relations. The changes in production relations have affected occupations. The experience requirements for using new tools for occupations will be further improved, and the occupational threshold will be further lowered. For example, before the advent of computers, engineering drawing/model building was basically done by hand. After the advent of computers, computers began to dominate drawing and model building, replacing traditional labor-intensive manual drawing. Prominent tools such as CAD began to spread the concept of computer-aided human design software. The requirements for occupations have also shifted from manual drawing experience to computer-aided drawing experience, but have the experienced manual draftsmen with rich experience been immediately eliminated? Obviously not, because tools only assist humans in improving production efficiency, but professional experience cannot be fully understood and mastered by novices in a short period of time, because the ultimate decision is made by humans, not assistive tools. Therefore, AI will improve production efficiency and lower some technical thresholds, but there will be no illiterate programmers. If there are illiterate "programmers", then they are definitely not programmers. It is just that AI has lowered the occupational threshold, allowing other professions to complete certain programming tasks.
    翻开人类工业革命史，每次技术革新，都把之前的技术工人淘汰了吗？然后并不是，技术革新带来了各类技术工具，使人的工作效率得到了大幅提升，生产力的发展进而促使了生产关系的变化，生产关系的变化又对职业产生了影响，职业会对新工具使用经验要求会进一步提升，同时职业门槛会进一步降低。举个例子：在计算机出来之前，工程制图/模型搭建基本都是手撸的，计算机出来之后，开始由计算机主导制图和模型搭建，代替了传统耗费工时手工制图，突出的工具例如 CAD 等等，以计算机辅助人类设计软件理念开始传播，对职业的要求也由手工制图经验向计算机制图经验开始转变，但之前丰富经验的手工制图师都立马淘汰了吗？很明显没有，因为工具只是辅助人类提升生产效率，但是职业化经验，并不是短时间新手就能完全理解和掌握的，因为最终做决策的是人，而不是辅助工具。因此 AI 会提高生产效率，会降低部分技术门槛，但不会存在什么文盲程序员，如果有文盲"程序员"，那绝对不是程序员，只是 AI 降低了职业门槛，使其他职业可以完成某些的编程任务罢了。
