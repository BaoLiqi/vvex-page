Should a novice Android developer give up?

菜鸟 Android 开发该放弃了吗？

I am an individual developer with a technology stack of Java, Kotlin, and Compose. I worked as a full-time developer in 2021, and then changed careers. However, I have continued to follow Android development. My Kotlin and Compose related libraries on GitHub have a total of over a thousand stars, and I consider myself to have a decent grasp of the relevant technologies. Last year, I almost crashed and burned after switching careers, so I was preparing to come back and continue developing. I looked at Boss Zhipin for a week and only applied for junior positions within Guangdong Province. I sent out almost 30 resumes, including some outsourcing companies that proactively asked for my resume. I didn't expect to not even get a single interview. Is it because I made a fatal mistake by changing careers/having too long of a gap, or is the internet client-side market really that cold? Should I continue to look for Android jobs, or should I switch careers? For example, switching to front-end React/NextJS? I am personally more familiar with these two, but I don't have any corporate project experience, so I really don't know how to find a job. And with the maturity of tools like v0 and Cursor, will the front-end become even more difficult than Android? Please give me some advice!

个人开发者，技术栈是 Java 、Kotlin 和 Compose 。21 年做过全职开发，之后就转行了，也有持续关注 Android 开发，GitHub 上 Kotlin 、Compose 相关的库一起大概有一千多的星，自认为相关技术掌握度还可以。 去年转行踩大雷几乎被搞垮准备回来继续做开发，看了一个星期的 Boss 直聘，只投了广东省内初级的，投了快 30 份简历，包括一些外包主动问我要简历的。没想到现在一个面试都没，是转行/空窗期太长犯了大忌还是互联网客户端真的这么凉了吗？ 该不该继续找安卓的，还是转行？比如转到前端的 React/NextJS ？个人对这两个比较熟一点，但是没有企业项目经验也属实不知道怎么找，而且 v0 、Cursor 之类的工具成熟之后前端会不会比 Android 更难？ 还请各位大佬指点指点！
