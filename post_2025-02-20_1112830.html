Let's define 'watching twice': it refers to seeing a movie in a theater by purchasing a ticket during its initial theatrical run. From my perspective, re-releases that occur years later don't count, like Titanic's rerelease (unless you watch them twice during the re-release, then it counts). Thinking back, I've probably only watched these twice: Inception Zootopia Dying to Survive Let the Bullets Fly (actually watched the Sichuan dialect version and the Mandarin version separately) Avatar 1 (watched 2D and 3D versions initially) Wolf Warrior 2 White Snake (watched this the second time to accompany a friend) The Mermaid Monkey King: Hero is Back Ne Zha 2
