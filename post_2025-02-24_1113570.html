Should a programmer with a monthly salary of 14k upgrade their Mac?

月薪 1.4w 的程序员，应该升级 Mac 吗？

I've been using an M1Pro 14-inch 16 + 512 for more than three years, and I'm thinking of getting the recently subsidized M4Pro with 48 + 1T. I do Go development and may do AI development in the future (connecting to AI interfaces, etc.). Actually, I know that what I have now can still be used, but the memory is a bit low, although it's never been full. But I don't dare to open Docker and virtual machines. If I had enough memory, I'd still like to play around with them. And the storage isn't enough, although I haven't filled it up, but that's on the condition that I keep deleting things. I have to admit that the desire to switch to a new one is also a consumer mentality. But even though I have a monthly salary of 14k, I actually have no savings and some loans. After the national subsidy, I still have to pay back 1.6k per month (12 interest-free installments). Should I buy it? Sigh. I feel itchy if I don't buy it. If I buy it, I won't be able to save any money.
