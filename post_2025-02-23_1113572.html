When I heard the Apple store employee say that using a phone case can affect battery life

Show Chinese Title

当听到苹果店员说，用手机壳会影响电池寿命

I happened to be passing by a direct store when it opened at 10 o'clock, and there was no one inside. The employee who came to greet me wanted to exchange pleasantries, so I complained that I had just updated the system, and the battery with 250 cycles had dropped from 100 to 99 and then to 98 today, with the entire cycle period increasing by no more than 10 times. The employee said, 'Oh, it has nothing to do with updating the system. According to our observation, using a phone case will reduce battery life.' I asked back, 'Because of accumulated heat?' The employee said yes, yes. I replied that if I didn't use a phone case, the battery life might still be there, but the phone life would be ruined if I dropped it. Then I heard them say, 'So we recommend purchasing AC.' I feel like this sales tactic has been trained for many years. I didn't believe it when others complained that updating the system reduced battery health before, but I really believe this method of reduction within 5 days.
