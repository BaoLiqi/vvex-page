When I heard the Apple store clerk say that using a phone case would affect battery life

闻苹果店员之言，护壳损电池寿，惑矣

When I was free, I passed by the direct store and it happened to be open at 10 o'clock. There was no one inside. The approaching clerk wanted to greet me, so I complained that after the system was updated, the battery with 250 cycles went from 100 to 99 and then to 98 today. The entire cycle period increased by no more than 10 times. The clerk said, "Oh, it has nothing to do with updating the system. According to our observation, using a phone case will reduce battery life." I asked back, "Because of accumulated heat?" The clerk said yes, yes. I replied that if I didn't use a phone case, I guess the battery life would still be there, but the phone life would be gone because I dropped it. Then I heard them say, "So we suggest buying AppleCare+." I feel that this sales pitch has been trained for many years. I didn't believe it when others complained that updating the system would reduce battery health before, but I really believe in this reduction method within 5 days.

偶经直营之处，适逢辰时启扉，店中空无一人。店员迎面而来，欲寒暄。余诉之，云系统骤新，电池历二百五十周期，初满，渐衰至九八，每轮增不过十次。店员曰：'此与新系无关，据吾等所察，护壳蔽体，损电池之寿数。' 余反问，因热聚乎？店员连声称是。余则反诘，若弃护壳，电池虽存，然机身早陨矣。继而闻之，'故荐君购AppleCare+'。觉此话术，演练积年。前闻人怨更新损电池，犹未信，然五日之内，骤减之速，不得不疑之矣。
