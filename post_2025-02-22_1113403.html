Feeling that macOS's interaction latency is poorly implemented

感觉 macos 的交互延迟做的很烂

I use macOS for work during the day and win at home for gaming and browsing forums at night. I always feel that Mac is a bit slow, with a sluggish feel, and typing always feels unsmooth, especially when writing documents~ Today, out of boredom, I specifically compared the input latency of the two systems. Mac: m2pro 32g system 15.3.1, win: win11, 13600kf Restart, randomly type in the Chrome tab bar, and press directly with all fingers on the keyboard. Divided into Chinese input method and pure English situations. win: Needless to say, it's absolutely instant, and you can't feel any lag at all. The result of mac is divided into two situations 1. abc English input status, there is almost no delay, at least I can't feel it. But for comparison, win is definitely faster. 2. Chinese input method status, I use WeChat input method, and I can feel the delay. After my finger leaves the keyboard for about 200ms, the screen responds. Not to mention the problem that the input method on Mac becomes more and more stuck after a long time of booting. Especially when too many Chrome windows are opened and the memory turns yellow and uses swap, the input delay can affect the rhythm of typing. The Mac's delay problem is also reflected in many system applications, such as the settings page, where you can quickly switch options with the mouse. On the Activity Monitor page, you can get stuck by quickly clicking back and forth on processes with the mouse.
