Using macOS for work during the day and Windows for gaming and browsing forums at home. I always feel that macOS is a step slower, with a sluggish feel, and typing is always not smooth, especially when writing documents. Divided into Chinese input method and pure English cases. win: Needless to say, it's absolutely instant, and there's no feeling of delay at all. Especially when too many chrome windows are opened and the memory turns yellow and is swapped, the input delay can affect the rhythm of typing. The delay problem of macOS is also reflected in many system applications, such as the settings page, switching options quickly with the mouse. In the Activity Monitor page, quickly clicking processes back and forth with the mouse can freeze itself.
