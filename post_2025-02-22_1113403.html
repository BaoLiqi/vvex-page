Feeling the interaction latency of macOS is terrible

Show Chinese Title

苹果机交互迟滞之感

Using macOS for work during the day and Windows for gaming and browsing forums at home. I always feel that macOS is a step slower, with a sluggish feel, and typing is always not smooth, especially when writing documents. Today, out of boredom, I specifically compared the input latency of the two systems. macOS: m2pro 32g system 15.3.1, win: win11, 13600kf After restarting, I typed randomly in the chrome tab bar, with all fingers placed directly on the keyboard. Divided into Chinese input method and pure English cases. win: Needless to say, it's absolutely instant, and there's no feeling of delay at all. The result of macOS is divided into two cases 1. abc English input state, almost no delay, at least I don't feel it. But to compare, Windows is definitely faster. 2. Chinese input method state, I use WeChat input method, and I can feel the delay, and it takes about 200ms for my fingers to leave the keyboard before the screen is fully responded. Not to mention that macOS has the problem of the input method becoming more and more laggy after being turned on for a long time. Especially when too many chrome windows are opened and the memory turns yellow and is swapped, the input delay can affect the rhythm of typing. The delay problem of macOS is also reflected in many system applications, such as the settings page, switching options quickly with the mouse. In the Activity Monitor page, quickly clicking processes back and forth with the mouse can freeze itself.
