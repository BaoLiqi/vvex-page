It's been almost a year since I became unemployed. My last job officially ended on March 15, 2024, and since then, I've been in a "free developer" state. I tried to find a job during this period, but there weren't many positions I could apply for. I only had one interview, and it didn't work out. So, starting in July, I devoted myself to independent development with high intensity—as long as the library was open (closed on Mondays), I spent almost the entire day there. After returning to my room at night, I sometimes continued working until midnight. My main focus is the crypto space, mainly doing airdrop hunting and arbitrage (originally, I was waiting for the zkSync results in June, but it wasn't ideal, so I officially started independent development in July). 💰 Timeline of turning income positive: ✅ October 2024: The script for launching new coins (Sui chain meme coin launch) started generating income. ✅ December 2024: Arbitrage (Solana chain arbitrage between various DEXs) started generating income. ✅ December 2024: Someone on X actively contacted me via private message, offering to sponsor me weekly to continue developing the arbitrage program (I also shared the code with him). ✅ February 2025: I received a part-time job writing some simple crypto bots. Among them, the income from launching new coins and arbitrage is unstable, but the "fixed salary" from sponsorship + part-time work is about 20,000+ per month. 💡 Projects tried in the past few months, focusing on "fast development, fast launch, fast trial and error, fast next": - Script to automatically mint Bitcoin Runes - Image processing website - Edited a few videos - Script to batch stake Cosmos - Twitter bot - Bot to boost Sui transaction volume / transaction user count - And a few websites that are "not very useful" 😂 - Etc... 📌 Current status: Comfortably at home, full-time "free development" (although the income is still not stable enough). 🚀 Future: Continue to work hard and strive to retire early. Short-term expectation: Find an arbitrage / Rust development partner in "reality" to share some of the workload!
