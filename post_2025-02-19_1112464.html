# Labor Arbitration Experience After more than half a year, it finally came to an end. Although the process was quite tortuous, the final result was still satisfactory. Here, I would like to sincerely thank everyone on V2EX who has helped me. Thank you all for your advice back then. I also thank V2EX for giving me the meaning of sharing. The following are some of my experiences, and I hope they can be helpful to everyone. ## July 28, 2024: Preparing for Labor Arbitration After unsuccessful communication with the company, I prepared for labor arbitration. At that time, as a newcomer to the workplace, I didn't even know what the specific details of the five insurances and one housing fund were, let alone labor arbitration. So I started seeking help from all parties. Fortunately, with the help of V2EX users, I followed the advice and applied for assistance from a lawyer at the legal aid center. Later, I also supplemented and collected evidence as suggested. ### About applying for a lawyer: You must have agricultural household registration and need to obtain a certificate of agricultural household registration nature, which can be issued by the local town police station. ## August 1, 2024: Signing an Agreement and Submitting an Arbitration Application After signing the agreement and power of attorney with the legal aid center, I started to connect with the lawyer. On August 3, I submitted the labor arbitration application to the arbitration commission. I filed the lawsuit with the arbitration commission under the jurisdiction of my work location, and I could also file a lawsuit with the arbitration commission at the company's registered location (the subsequent trouble lies in this area). A week later, I received a notice of trial, tentatively scheduled for mid-August. During this period, I discussed the details with the lawyer and prepared a statement of defense. ### August 14: First Hearing On the day of the hearing, none of the relevant personnel of the company showed up, so I could only submit evidence unilaterally. The judge asked me to provide proof that I worked in Xicheng. At that time, I only had the DingTalk attendance records, which were saved as screenshots. The judge did not approve of it and asked me to show the "original carrier" of the evidence. However, the company kicked me out of the DingTalk group chat three days after notifying me, so there was no enterprise certification on DingTalk, and the judge did not approve the evidence. **Suggestion**: Be sure to keep relevant evidence in a timely manner. It is best to use video recording software with watermarks to keep the evidence in video form. Later, I showed the judge the chat records of the WeChat work group. WeChat also did not have enterprise certification and could not prove my employment relationship with the company. Therefore, the judge suggested that we file a lawsuit with the arbitration commission at the company's registered location due to the issue of jurisdiction. The lawyer was also a little confused. He brought me a lot of evidence, but the judge did not approve of it. Finally, the judge gave us two choices: submit a lawsuit to the company's registered location or wait for the next hearing. After communicating with the lawyer, I chose to wait for the next hearing, because if I re-submitted the lawsuit, I would have to go to another district to re-apply for a lawyer or go by myself. ### Complaint: When filing the case, a screenshot of the proof of work location is acceptable, but I donâ€™t know why it is not accepted during the hearing. ## September 3, 2024: Second Hearing After half a month of waiting, the second hearing came. During this period, I and the lawyer kept communicating on how to prove the work location, but coincidentally, the company sent someone over for the second time. The judge asked him if he admitted that I worked in Xicheng, and he answered in the affirmative. In this way, the evidence that had troubled me for half a month was glossed over. The subsequent process proceeded normally, with both parties presenting evidence to each other. The person sent by the company was my previous leader. I don't know if he thought the company would definitely win or what, but he recognized most of the evidence I presented, and he didn't even calculate my compensation amount before approving it. But later, when it came time for him to present evidence, he was dumbfounded. I did not approve of most of his evidence. On the key evidence, the "Termination of Labor Contract Notice", the company failed to provide strong evidence to prove my fault. Instead, he presented evidence to the judge on some irrelevant aspects, such as: not participating in company training, not obeying management, and frequently asking for leave (attending conference live training on DingTalk during working hours, and using my own time off is also considered asking for leave? What is the reason for management in an employment relationship?). The judge denied his evidence without asking me if I admitted it. Because the company did not attend the first hearing, it was not allowed to submit additional evidence for the second time. Later, the judge even said sarcastically: If you really recognized this evidence, why didn't you write it in the termination notice at that time? The rest was the usual procedure, and the judge persuaded both sides to mediate. Unexpectedly, the company chose not to mediate. ## October 14, 2024: The Ruling Comes Down After a month and a half, the ruling finally came down. When I received the ruling, I really felt like the hard days were over. In the end, it was ruled as a final ruling, with 2N compensation. Since the amount was not large, the ruling was a final ruling, and the company could no longer appeal, but it could apply to the intermediate court to revoke the ruling. I was also very happy and sent a pennant to the lawyer. I thought I would get the money smoothly, but the company kept dragging it on... ## December 18, 2024: Applying for Compulsory Enforcement After waiting for 45 days, the company did not apply to the intermediate court to revoke the ruling, nor did it compensate me. So I prepared to apply for compulsory enforcement. Fortunately, compulsory enforcement can be done online, without having to run around offline, which is relatively convenient. The process of compulsory enforcement is like going through labor arbitration again. You have to make various phone calls to consult and submit materials, etc. After several rejections, the case was successfully filed online on December 18, 2024. I feel that this process is quite torturous, because even if you win the labor arbitration, it does not mean that you can really get the money. I donâ€™t know if the waiting process is meaningful. During this period, my father called. I didn't want to tell them at first, but the truth cannot be concealed forever. My father is a farmer and has more traditional ideas. He still lives in the era when officials are the most important (it doesn't seem wrong to say that now). He scolded me for why I didnâ€™t do a good job and had to sue people and fight the company. How could we possibly win against them? Due to repeated delays, I also began to wonder if I could really get the money, was it really right to do this, and was it meaningful. ## January 3, 2025: Successful Enforcement, Received Compensation I didn't have any ideas about finding a job at the time, and even had a certain resistance to work. Later, when I communicated with many classmates, I found that they had also been laid off. We, as fresh graduates, were not having a good time. The classmates said that the country would solve the employment problem and would give subsidies to companies that hired fresh graduates. The company would try to "remove" our fresh graduate status. In this way, with the idea of â€‹â€‹lying down at home rather than being internally conflicted outside, I returned home before the New Year. After returning home, I felt much happier, and good news followed. After communicating with the enforcement judge several times, he decided to carry out enforcement on-site on January 3. Fortunately, I provided the judge with several accurate phone numbers, and the enforcement process went smoothly (it is recommended that you keep relevant personnel information as soon as possible and make plans early). I got the money very smoothly a few days ago. The matter is also coming to an end. Currently, I am learning while looking for a job, learning more technical stacks, enriching my skills, and hoping to find a satisfactory job soon. I also wish you all the best and have a good future!
