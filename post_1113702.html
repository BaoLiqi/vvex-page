My friend has some technical aspirations, but everyone understands the bank's technology. Although there are new technologies, there are more old things. The current total package is around 300,000 yuan, but since she has always been a marginal person, a transparent figure, and the work assigned is not very important, her performance has been consistently rated as poor. In addition, my friend also mentioned that she is not used to the workplace atmosphere in the bank, such as learning to actively express herself, ask for credit, etc., so she is considering finding another job. My friend interviewed for an outsourcing position in a major Internet company and could get a 30% salary increase on the current income and do more technically related content, rather than doing more management-oriented work like in the bank, so she is very excited and plans to quit her job without a new one lined up and prepare for a while to see if there's any better opportunity. I dissuaded her, thinking it would be more appropriate to look for a better job while still employed. But my friend wants to pursue her ideals, so I didn't try to persuade her further.
