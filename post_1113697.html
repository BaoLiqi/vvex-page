Guys, recommend a router

大佬们推荐个路由器

I got a 500M Telecom broadband on the weekend. The optical modem is in the living room, two walls and 5m away from my room. The 2.4G wifi can connect, but the internet speed is very poor. It takes a long time to load videos. The 5G wifi cannot connect at all. Now I want to buy a gigabit router. I looked at ZTE's Xuntian BE5100, ZTE's Xuntian BE5100pro+, and ZTE's Xuntian AX3000pro+. I don't know if it is enough to buy ZTE's Xuntian BE5100. The comments say its wall penetration is average.

周末拉了个 500M 电信宽带，光猫在客厅，离我的房子有两堵墙，5m 远，2.4G 的那个 wifi 可以连上，但是网速贼差，刷视频都要卡半天，5G 那个 wifi 根本连不上 现在想买个千兆路由器，看了一下中兴巡天版 be5100 、中兴巡天版 be5100pro+、中兴巡天版 ax3000pro+。不知道买中兴巡天版 be5100 够不够用，评论说穿墙一般

  • I think Wi-Fi should not be used through walls if possible. Use the router itself or a wireless router with DHCP turned off to connect to the LAN port to cover frequently used areas; this will better guarantee speed, signal, and mobile device battery life. Specifically in the case of the poster, you can run a network cable from the living room to the room where you use the Internet, and then buy a wireless router that supports Wi-Fi 6 (axXXXX), turn off DHCP in the settings, and then connect the network cable to any LAN port. In this way, the new router can supplement the coverage that the optical modem Wi-Fi does not cover, but unless the price difference is not big in frequently used areas, there is no need to buy a Wi-Fi 7 (beXXXX) router at this stage, and mobile devices have not yet been popularized.
    我认为 Wi-Fi 有条件还是不要穿墙使用，经常使用的区域用路由器本体或者关掉 DHCP 的无线路由器接 LAN 口来覆盖；这样速度、信号、移动设备续航都有更好的保证具体到楼主的情况，可以从客厅拉一根网线到你上网的房间，再买个支持 wi-fi 6 (axXXXX) 的无线路由器，设置里关掉 DHCP 然后网线接任意 LAN 口。这样的话新路由器可以补充覆盖光猫 Wi-Fi 覆盖不到的，但经常使用的区域除非价格差别不大，现阶段无需买 Wi-Fi 7 (beXXXX) 的路由器，移动设备这边还没有普及
