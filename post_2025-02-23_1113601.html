Let each person give a life advice

一个人给一个人生建议吧

It increasingly feels like many paths are easier for those who come later because others have explored them. If you suddenly realize something at some point in your life that helped you, or if there's something you regret not trying sooner, feel free to write it down here. It might be helpful to others. I'll start: I deeply regret sleeping too much in college, to the point where I sometimes feel like I didn't leave anything behind in my youth.

越发觉得很多路是因为有了其他人探索过，才方便了后来人。 如果你生命某个时刻的忽然领悟到帮助了你，或者是某个时间点开始懊恼没有及时尝试的事情，不妨在这里写下，或许也能对其他人有些帮助。 我先来： 很后悔自己大学时候睡了太多觉，以至于某些时刻会恍惚觉得青春什么也没留下。
