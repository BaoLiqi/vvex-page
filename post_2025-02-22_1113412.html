I plan not to cancel the Mobile account for now and directly apply for China Unicom broadband. I will use this Mobile broadband to run a PCDN to offset the remaining months of broadband fees until the contract expires (I will upload like crazy, and Mobile may beg me to cancel the account at that time). The current setup at home is optical modem bridging - soft router dialing - switch connected to various devices below. I want to move directly to the China Unicom optical modem and no longer connect to the Mobile optical modem (there are not enough interfaces to connect to anyway). Now I feel that a better solution is to connect a separate device to the Mobile optical modem, but now I don't know how to minimize the cost.
